who are we

Welcome to boba^Lab, where mixology meets molecular gastronomy to redefine the boba experience.
Step into our modernist alcoholic boba bar, where we put a unique twist on traditional boba drinks.
We believe that cocktails should be an experience, a fusion of art and science.

We don't just serve drinks; we craft memories.

Our bartenders are not just mixologists but artists, passionately dedicated to delivering a memorable and immersive experience.
Through meticulous craftsmanship, we transform carefully selected ingredients into a sensory narrative.
Each element holds its own story, sourced for quality and cultural significance.

With measured precision, we blend these elements, creating a symphony of flavors that transports you on a captivating journey. Every sip tells a tale, inviting you to savor the rich narratives woven into our thoughtfully crafted cocktails.
Allow us to guide you through an enchanting expedition of exquisite flavors and captivating sensations.

Here's to pushing the boundaries of boba, crafting molecular cocktails that redefine the art of mixology, one sip at a time.

— Will

Founder and CEO

Alchemy Lab

Spherified Fruit Martini

A visually stunning martini where fruits like strawberries, melons, or grapes are transformed into flavorful gelatinous spheres, suspended in a clear vodka or gin base.

Edible Mist Martini

A molecular twist on the classic martini, where the cocktail is served in a glass filled with a flavorful edible mist on a bed of smoky char siu cotton candy that adds a burst of aroma and taste with every sip.

Frozen Nitro Oolong Tea

A frozen milk tea concoction infused with the smoothness of liquid nitrogen, resulting in a velvety texture and a dramatic, billowing vapor effect when served.

Boba Bubble Blast

A milk tea-based cocktail infused with carbonation, creating fizzy boba bubbles that burst with delightful flavors as you sip.

Levitating Citrus Fizz

A whimsical concoction featuring levitating citrus slices suspended in a sparkling gin and tonic, achieved through the use of magnetic levitation techniques.

Molecular Taro Delight

A milk tea cocktail featuring taro-infused milk, taro-flavored boba pearls, and taro-flavored gel cubes, providing a multi-layered and indulgent experience.
